SilentCue

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

The Short Version

SilentCue does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Ever.

What We Collect

Nothing. SilentCue has no user accounts, no registration, no sign-in, and no network connection of any kind. The app never contacts a server.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your presentation duration, milestone settings, and saved profiles are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults mechanism. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party SDKs and Analytics

SilentCue contains no third-party SDKs, no analytics frameworks, no crash reporting services, and no advertising networks. The only frameworks used are Apple's own: SwiftUI, CoreHaptics, WatchConnectivity, and WidgetKit.

Permissions

SilentCue requests no system permissions. You will never be asked to grant access to your microphone, camera, location, contacts, or any other system resource.

Children's Privacy

SilentCue does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. There is nothing to opt out of.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Given that the app collects no data, we do not anticipate meaningful changes.
